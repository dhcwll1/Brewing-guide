This is a guide for brewing potions in Minecraft

Full guide about obtaining ingredients, and brewing potions


What is a potion?

A potion is an item, giving the player effects, which can modify their abilities.

How can you use them?

You can use them in many ways. You can use them on yourself, or throw it at your teammates to heal them. You can also make harming potions, which you can throw at your enemies, or if you are weird, you can drink them too.

Obtaining ingredients

If you want to brew potions, you will have to get ingredients for it by yourself

Most ingredients are found in The Nether

Ingredients and their obtainment

Magma Cream
obtaining: Craft from blaze powder and slimeball

Glistering Melon
obtaining: Craft melon slice with gold nuggets

Golden Carrot
obtaining: Craft with carrot and 8 gold nuggets

Fermented Spider Eye
obtaining: Craft with spider eye, brown mushroom, and sugar

Rabbit's Foot
obtaining: Rare drop from rabbits

Spider Eye
obtaining: Drop from spiders

Ghast Tear
obtaining: Drop from ghasts in the Nether

Phantom Membrane
obtaining: Drop from phantoms

Sugar
obtaining: Crafted from sugar cane

Blaze Powder
obtaining: Crafted from blaze rods (Nether)

Pufferfish
obtaining: Caught while fishing
