Recipes

These are the recipes for potions in minecraft

Potion of Fire Resistance
Ingredients: Magma Cream
Effect: Immunity to fire and lava

Potion of Healing
Ingredients: Glistering Melon
Effect: Instantly restores health

Potion of Invisibility
Ingredients: Golden Carrot + Fermented Spider Eye
Effect: Makes player invisible
Does not make armor and in-hand items invisible

Potion of Leaping
Ingredients: Rabbit's Foot
Effect: Higher jumps

Potion of Night Vision
Ingredients: Golden Carrot
Effect: Improved vision in the dark

Potion of Poison
Ingredients: Spider Eye
Effect: Deals poison damage over time
Recommended to use gunpowder

Potion of Regeneration
Ingredients: Ghast Tear
Effect: Restores health over time

Potion of Slow Falling
Ingredients: Phantom Membrane
Effect: Slows falling speed, prevents fall damage

Potion of Slowness
Ingredients: Sugar + Fermented Spider Eye
Effect: Reduces movement speed
Recommended to use gunpowder

Potion of Strength
Ingredients: Blaze Powder
Effect: Increases melee damage

Potion of Swiftness
Ingredients: Sugar
Effect: Increases movement speed

Potion of Water Breathing
Ingredients: Pufferfish
Effect: Allows breathing underwater

Potion of Weakness
Ingredients: Fermented Spider Eye
Effect: Reduces attack damage
Recommended to use gunpowder
Do not make an awkward potion for this, put it directly into water

Potion of Harming
Ingredients: Potion of Healing or Poison + Fermented Spider Eye
Effect: Instantly deals damage
Recommended to use gunpowder

Potion of Decay
Ingredients: Wither Rose (Bedrock only)
Effect: Applies the Wither effect

Potion of Luck
Ingredients: Unobtainable in survival
Effect: Increases luck (Java only, commands)
